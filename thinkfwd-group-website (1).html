FAQ

Common questions.

i. How is ThinkFWD Group different from McKinsey or the Big Four? +

The Big Four deliver AI strategy at enterprise scale with enterprise pricing and enterprise timelines. We work with mid-market companies ($50M to $500M in revenue) who need the same rigor without the overhead. Our frameworks are shaped by hands-on product work inside regulated environments, not by aggregating junior associate slideware. And we stay deep in the AI vendor landscape specifically so we can match the right technology to the problem you are solving, at your scale, in your industry.

ii. How do you help us choose the right AI technology and vendor? +

The AI platform market is vast, fragmented, and moving fast. What fits a $400M health system is wrong for a $75M SaaS company, and vice versa. Our job is to stay current on the vendor landscape so we can match the right technology to the problem you are actually solving, at your scale, within your industry and regulatory context. Every recommendation is anchored to your Readiness Assessment score and the opportunities we prioritized together. When implementation is needed, we help you select the right partner and oversee the build through Phase 02 of our service model.

iii. What if we already have an AI vendor or internal team in place? +

That is often when our work has the highest leverage. Most organizations bring us in mid-stream, when the technology is moving but results are not. The Assessment identifies what changed, what the original strategy missed, and how to realign the work to the business case. We do not replace vendors or teams. We make sure their effort produces the outcome you were promised.

iv. What happens in the first 30 days? +

The AI Readiness Assessment is conducted in the first week through a structured 45 to 60 minute conversation with executive stakeholders. You receive a written report that includes a scored readiness profile, an ROI model built on your actual numbers, and a clear recommendation on next steps. Weeks two through four are used for deeper discovery, workflow mapping, and opportunity prioritization. The output of the first 30 days is a roadmap ready for board presentation.
